FAQs

For who is the app for?

In fact, the ComuNeedy application is intended for the entire population, but the application was established mainly because of thinking about helpless people, such as elders, who need basic help in their daily routines. For example, assembling pieces of furniture, changing light bulbs, helping to carry groceries home, etc.

How does the volunteering functionality work?

It is very simple! The user upload a request for help and the request pops up for volunteer users filtered by the city that the request was uploaded. The volunteer submits the request and gets the user's details to reach a contact with him.

How can I trust the volunteer

Our system provides rating functionality in addition for verification that one of the admins commits in front of a new volunteer that register to the app. After each volunteering the user gets the option to give a rating for the volunteer. For low and bad feedbacks the volunteer will be blocked from the system.

How much does it cost to use the volunteering community system?

Our volunteering community system operates entirely free of charge. There are no costs associated with signing up as a user, becoming an admin, or volunteering your time to help others. We believe in fostering a spirit of generosity and community service without financial barriers. Our platform is sustained by the goodwill and contributions of individuals like you who believe in the power of helping others without expecting anything in return

Is there a dedicated space for users to ask questions and seek assistance within the volunteering community platform?

Absolutely! We've incorporated a user-friendly forum where individuals can freely ask questions, seek advice, and share knowledge within our volunteering community. This forum serves as a central hub for users to connect, engage in meaningful discussions, and support one another in their volunteering endeavors. Whether you're looking for guidance on a specific project, seeking recommendations, or simply want to connect with like-minded individuals, our forum provides a welcoming space for open dialogue and collaboration. Join the conversation and tap into the collective wisdom of our community to enhance your volunteering experience!
